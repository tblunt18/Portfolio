Black Twitter Research

The Black Twitter Research Project is a current research initiative at Spelman College. I am part of a two person team that is working to build a tool to showcase our research. The website will provide background and data, as well as an interactive playground. In the near future, the playground will include an interactive map of the tweets that we have streamed and interactive word cloud generator, word analysis tool, and hashtag prediction generator. Click to enter the site Black Twitter Research Site

DSSG ADT Tool

As a DSSG Fellow, my primary mission was to help increase the accessibility of important data. I helped build a mapping tool to display what Westside community member eligibility looks like through the lens of the Anti Displacementn Tax Fund. Click to enter the site ADT Eligibility Tool
